translationCore

A free, open-source desktop workspace that keeps your translation team focused on accurate, clear, and natural Scripture.

translationCore keeps the unfoldingWord translation helps beside your project so you can review every verse together, capture feedback, and stay ready to publish.

View on GitHub

Built for church-based translation teams

translationCore keeps everyone aligned on the Gateway Language Strategy by guiding teams through the same checks used around the world.

  • Follow guided translation checks that focus your attention verse by verse.
  • Keep comments, issues, and decisions together so teammates always know what comes next.
  • Work offline in the field and sync updates with Door43 or your own Git repository when you are back online.

The translation helps you need are included

translationNotes

See common issues for every verse and record how your team resolved them.

translationQuestions

Use comprehension questions to confirm the meaning is clear to first-time hearers.

translationWords

Check key terms for consistency and keep definitions available without an internet connection.

translationAcademy

Learn the principles behind church-based Bible translation with practical training articles.

Get started in three simple steps

  1. Download the latest release

    Choose the installer for Windows, macOS, or Linux and sign in to your existing Door43 account if you have one.

  2. Open or create a project

    Import content from Door43, unfoldingWord, or local files and invite teammates to review the same translation.

  3. Check Scripture together

    Walk through each resource, capture feedback, and finish with a project that is ready to publish.
